Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 22, 2026

1. Zero Data Collection Guarantee

VeilCast is designed from the ground up to protect your privacy. Our core philosophy is that your data belongs to you. Therefore, the VeilCast application does not collect, transmit, store, or monitor any personal data, usage telemetry, or screen contents.

2. Fully Offline Architecture

VeilCast is a standalone native application that operates entirely offline on your local machine. It does not require an internet connection to function, and it contains no analytics or tracking software (such as Google Analytics or crash reporters).

3. Local Storage

The only data VeilCast saves is your personal configuration preferences (such as window states and theme settings). These preferences are stored locally in a secure `.vcfg` file on your hard drive and are never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

4. Changes to this Policy

Because VeilCast does not collect data, this privacy policy is highly unlikely to change. However, should any updates occur, they will be posted on this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our official GitHub repository.
